Our Research Framework

From Announcement to Measurable Outcomes

Every Outcome Media investigation follows the same six-stage evaluation framework.

01

Announcement

Official public statements, declarations and agreements.

02

Commitment

Specific commitments made by governments or institutions.

03

Institutional Mechanism

Committees, agreements and implementation structures.

04

Implementation

Policies, investments and projects that entered execution.

05

Observable Outcome

Publicly measurable results supported by evidence.

06

Attribution

Assessment of whether outcomes can reasonably be linked to the original commitments.
